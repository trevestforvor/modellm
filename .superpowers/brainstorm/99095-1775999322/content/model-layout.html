Trade-offs

Grid Cards

  • Visually distinctive — feels premium
  • Good with 2-6 models
  • Gets awkward with 10+ models (lots of scrolling)
  • Less info density per model
  • Your preference from earlier

Compact List

  • Scales to any number of models
  • More info visible at a glance
  • What every competitor does
  • Feels more utilitarian
  • Better for quick model switching
